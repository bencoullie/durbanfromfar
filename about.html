About

Artist

Solo work

Durban From Far is currently comprised of me alone - Ben Coullie (23). I started the project to practice and improve on soundtrack-inspired composition and that's just what I'll do.

I collect soundtracks; from obscure indie games to hollywood films I've been slowly amassing my own personal library which (as well as giving me feels) provides a massive chunk of my inspiration.

In late 2013 I moved from Durban, South Africa to Auckland , New Zealand (LOTR awww yus).

Ultimately I want to compose soundtracks. Sounds clich√©, but I don't care about being paid for it.

About

method

Digital

90% of what I do is via a DAW (digital audio workstation). Renoise is the one I use most often.

Ideally I would have people play the instruments but, as orchestras are hard to come by, I make do with digital sounds.

Although I'm still learning myself, I'll be more than happy to help anyone wanting to understand the basics. Just email me!

Instrumental

I love to play around with different instruments and figure out ways of including them in my music.

I primarily play ukulele but I also dabble in guitar, piano and pretty much anything else I can get my hands on.

I plan to incorporate more instrumental stuff as I get better (don't hold your breath).
